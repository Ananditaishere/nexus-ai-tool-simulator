METHODOLOGY

How the engine thinks

01

Task mapping

Each of 8 task categories maps to a curated set of AI tools scored by domain experts across 6 parameters.

02

Weighted scoring

Your priority sliders become multipliers. Score = Σ(param_score × weight) / max_possible × 100.

03

Requirement boosts

Special requirements (privacy, free tier, API) dynamically adjust relevant tool scores upward when applicable.

04

Ranked output

Tools are sorted by final weighted score. Top pick is highlighted with a full breakdown per parameter.
